Nominations & Submissions

How do I submit my work for nomination?

To submit your project, navigate to the Nominations tab while logged into a verified account. Provide the direct public link to your content (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, Behance, or personal portfolio), select the matching creative category, and provide a short overview description. Once submitted, you will receive an automated validation message checking your queue position.

Can I submit entries on behalf of another creator?

Yes, external nominations are permitted provided you have explicit, verifiable permission from the original asset holder. If an entry is flagged or disputed by the actual creator, the application will be immediately taken down pending authentication matching.

What happens to my content rights if I get nominated?

You retain 100% ownership of your work. By submitting, you simply grant us a royalty-free license to feature snippets or display links to your project on our voting page and live stream so global fans can review your entry.

Voting System & Safety

How often can I vote for an entry?

To ensure fair and integer distribution, our system limits voting intervals per category. Standard accounts can cast one verified vote per category every 24 hours. Automated scripts or multi-tab refreshing will trigger localized system blockades.

Why was an entry or profile disqualified?

Our backend systems actively monitor database submission patterns. Any entry showing artificial traffic bursts, bot farms, coordinated scripts, or purchased engagement metrics is automatically flagged and reviewed for immediate disqualification to protect community integrity.

Account & Verification

Why does my account say "loading..." or block access?

If your user dashboard displays a persistent loading screen, your internet connectivity might be delaying initialization with our Firebase safety nodes. Clear your browser storage metrics or perform a hardware reset to instantly fix your session token authentication.

How do I permanently delete my platform records?

Navigate directly to your Account settings dashboard and scroll to the bottom Danger Zone block. Clicking "Delete My Account" will instantly execute a full drop sequence, wiping your auth records and profile variables completely from our systems.
