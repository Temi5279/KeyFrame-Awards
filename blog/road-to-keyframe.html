Road to Keyframe: Pre-Nomination Guide & Selection Process Revealed

The stage is set, our systems are live, and the countdown to the creative event of the year has officially begun. Mark your calendars: Nominations open on November 4, 2026, and the grand finale takes place on January 9, 2027!

To help creators present their best work, the official timeline details how the submission pipeline will flow in the lead-up to November's open bracket.

What to Prepare Before November 4

To ensure your visual entry is processed smoothly the moment the nomination portal goes online, we recommend compiling your creative assets ahead of time:

  • Identify Your Best Work: Gather your highest-performing or most technically polished edit, stream highlight, or visual production.
  • Secure the Direct Link: Submissions must point to active, public URLs hosted on TikTok, YouTube Shorts, or Instagram Reels.
  • Select Your Focus Category: Look through our community categories on the nominations page to choose the perfect bracket for your style.
"Our process focuses purely on technical execution, visual flow, and editing prowess. We are not evaluating metrics; we are celebrating the craft."

The Nomination Selection Process

To keep the competition balanced, Keyframe Awards employs a structured verification filter. After submissions close, our validation team screens each database entry to verify category alignment and media format availability.

Once verified, the curated shortlist moves directly to the global community voting dashboard starting December 20—giving audiences the power to decide who claims the title on January 9.

Prepare your links, polish your sequences, and get ready to secure your spot when the portal opens on November 4.
