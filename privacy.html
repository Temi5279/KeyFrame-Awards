OPERATIONAL COMPLIANCE & GOVERNANCE

PRIVACY POLICY

Effective Date: June 20, 2026. This comprehensive legal framework outlines the strict data protection protocols, extraction limits, and user safety metrics enforced across The Keyframe Awards Network.

1. Data Collection & Device Authentication

To ensure a completely fair, transparent, and anti-cheat voting experience for global creator fandoms, our platform utilizes device-locked verification tokens. When you cast a ballot, we log encrypted, anonymous hardware identifiers (such as hashed IP headers and browser agent metadata) to prevent automated bot scripts, farm accounts, and spam scripts from manipulating live leaderboards. We do not harvest personal phone logs, contacts, geolocation coordinates, or local device storage data.

2. Nomination Link Parsing & Media Curation

When fans or creators submit links via our Nomination Portal, we only process the public metadata provided by third-party APIs (such as public video URLs, channel names, view metrics, and creator handles from YouTube or TikTok). We do not store private user profiles, passwords, or account credentials from these external social platforms. All submitted media handles are used solely for curation evaluation and category placement.

3. Third-Party Embeds and Platform Services

Our platform references and links directly to official broadcast hubs on external networks (specifically TikTok Live and YouTube Live). Please be aware that when you click out to these external applications or interact with embedded media feeds, your interaction is governed by the respective privacy policies of Google LLC and ByteDance Ltd. The Keyframe Awards Network does not control or maintain telemetry tracking operated by these external networks.

4. Cookies, Sessions, & Traffic Analytics

We implement micro-cookie protocols and localized web storage tokens to preserve your structural preferences (such as remembering active UI states) and to maintain stable session connectivity during high-bitrate live streaming events. These telemetry layers monitor regional traffic spikes and server loads, allowing our engineering team to dynamically allocate bandwidth resources during heavy real-time voting waves.

5. Data Retention & Purge Schedules

We believe in data minimization. All anonymous hardware tokens and verification hashes collected during the active voting window are strictly temporary. Following the official broadcast and closing ceremonies in January, all voting logs, bot mitigation data, and device history tokens are permanently scrubbed and purged from our production databases within 90 days.

6. International Compliance & Children's Privacy

As a global entertainment ecosystem, we respect user safety. The Keyframe Awards Network does not knowingly collect, request, or maintain identifiable information from individuals under the age of 13. If our administrative panel discovers that any unauthorized database ingestion has occurred, the data is immediately flagged, isolated, and permanently deleted from our servers.

7. Security Infrastructure & Encryption Standard

Your security is non-negotiable. All network transmissions occurring on this platform are heavily guarded via Secure Socket Layer (SSL/TLS) encryption layers. Collected data tokens sit behind secure, isolated network firewalls. We maintain a strict zero-monetization policy: your interaction history is confidential and is never sold, leased, or distributed to third-party advertising companies or marketing algorithms.

8. Modifications to this Policy

The Keyframe Awards Network reserves the operational right to update or adjust this compliance framework as new features, voting protocols, or custom streaming engines are introduced. Any structural updates will be cleanly logged right here with a revised effective date.
