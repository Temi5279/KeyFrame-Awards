OUR MISSION

The Keyframe Awards is the premier global evaluation platform built to celebrate exceptional digital artistry, high-velocity video editing, and community-driven storytelling. We bridge the gap between under-the-radar creators and massive global fandoms.

The Global Battleground

Every single day, millions of elite creators shape internet culture through high-end mobile edits, complex color grading, and razor-sharp transition work. Yet, major traditional media award systems completely ignore them. We designed this network to validate their talent, handing the absolute power over to verified device-locked community votes.

The Competitive Disciplines

Football Curation

2D Artwork & VFX
