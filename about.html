Behind The Frame

The story, the passion, and the mission driving the Keyframe community.

Our Vision

In the digital age, video content moves the world—yet the mastermind editors, motion graphic designers, and typography artists behind the scenes rarely get the mainstream credit they deserve. The Keyframe Awards was built to change that story.

We are establishing the ultimate online platform dedicated entirely to honoring creators who turn ordinary timelines into masterfully synced, frame-by-frame works of art. From complex kinetic typography lyric videos to custom-built hardware production shots, we provide the stage for creators to earn their digital flowers.

The Roadmap

The event is designed to celebrate the community transparently through a structural multi-phase approach:

1. Submissions

Nominations officially launch on November 4. Users and creators submit their top video links across our main award categories.

2. Community Vote

Once categories are finalized, authenticated users unlock live dashboard ballots to lock in votes for their favorite creators.

3. The Ceremony

We conclude with a high-energy live-stream celebration broadcast, announcing the official winners and showcasing top project files.
