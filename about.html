What is Keyframe?

Redefining how we honor the architects of digital visual art.

Our Origin

In the digital landscape, the most engaging moments are driven entirely by post-production artistry. Whether it's a seamless football skills montage, a beautifully timed K-pop edit, a chaotic live stream replay, or a viral meme that captures global attention, the technical and creative work behind these assets is often overlooked.

The Keyframe Awards was built to change that narrative. We created a platform to give visual creators, sync coordinators, animators, and independent content editors their long-overdue industry validation.

The Mission

"To decentralize award culture by providing a transparent, interactive, and community-driven space where the technical skill and creative execution of digital creators take center stage."
