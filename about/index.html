About The Awards

The Keyframe Awards is the premier digital stage built exclusively to honor editors, motion designers, animators, and visual creators who shape modern internet culture.

For too long, the brilliant minds behind the screen have been left out of mainstream award ceremonies. We are changing that by giving digital post-production technicians the primary spotlight they deserve.
